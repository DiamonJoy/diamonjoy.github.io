Biography

Bin Xu (许彬) is a algorithm engineer at Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences. His research focuses on AI for Science, Spatio-Temporal Data Analysis, Large Language Model and Computer Vision.

Xu was an algorithm expert at Youdao AI Lab. Xu involved himself in products on Youdao Smart Hardware and Youdao AI Cloud.

Xu received his M.S. degree and B.S. degree from Beijing Institute of Technology, China, in 2018 and 2015.

Xu was awarded the The CCF Outstanding Undergraduate Award in 2014 and Beijing Merit Student in 2013.
